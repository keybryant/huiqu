ABOUT COMPANY
YiFang Fun (Shenzhen) Technology Co., Ltd. was established in March 2021. The company is composed of professional Internet and AR technical experts, top trendy toy design team, graphic design team and a professional mold manufacturing factory with leading technology.
The company is committed to the design and production of new trendy cement-free construction toys designed by Augmented Reality technology and advanced mold manufacturing technology; it is built around four areas of IP cooperative operation, consumer reach, popular culture promotion and cultivation, to make a new integrated operation platform for trendy toys.
Company Culture
The Company philosophy "Hobby for Fun" guides us to make products admixtures of YiFang essence and fun,letting customers gain knowledge and joy from the process of building YIFANG models and being immersed in the brand-new joyful experience brought by YIFANG products.
The company combines manual assembly with AR technology and creates new trendy toys. The sensory experience of product use is enhanced further by interesting building, collection, education and social interactions in modern life.
